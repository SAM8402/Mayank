🍫 Sweet Facts About Chocolate

🍫

Ancient Origins

Chocolate dates back over 3,000 years to the ancient Maya and Aztec civilizations, who considered it the "food of the gods."

💛

Happiness Chemical

Chocolate contains phenylethylamine, the same chemical your brain produces when you fall in love. No wonder it makes us happy!

🌍

World's Favorite

Over 7.2 million tons of chocolate are consumed worldwide each year. Switzerland leads per capita consumption.

💎

White Isn't Really

White chocolate isn't technically chocolate — it contains no cocoa solids, only cocoa butter, sugar, and milk.

Hug Day

The Power of a Hug

A warm embrace can heal what words cannot. It speaks the language of the heart, crossing every barrier and filling the soul with comfort.

Research shows that a 20-second hug releases oxytocin, the "love hormone," reducing stress and strengthening the bond between two people.

"Sometimes, all you need is a hug from the right person and all your stress will melt away."

🤰

The Bear Hug

Tight and all-encompassing. Says "I never want to let you go."

👫

The Side Hug

Casual and sweet. Walking side by side, connected always.

🤲

The Comforting Hug

Gentle and protective. "I'm here for you, no matter what."

👬

The Long Hug

Lingering and meaningful. When seconds turn into moments of pure love.
